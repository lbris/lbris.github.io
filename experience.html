Zombie Dice

As a final to the AP Computer Science year I completed a project that replicates the game of Zombie Dice. This program showed my skills working with JAVA and how well I could organize the coding behind it. It challenged me with random number generation, many loops, arrays and objects. I ended up receiving an 'A' on the project and a better understanding of the language.

Current Projects

In Computer Programming IV, I have completed a handful of very challenging programs, including a Bingo game that exports and imports cards from text files, a perfect card shuffling program that can both in-shuffle and out-shuffle, and a program that records statistics, transforms that data to averages and point values, and stores it in a text file. All of these programs have further advanced my knowledge of programming, and have given me a chance to show the extent of what I am really capable.
